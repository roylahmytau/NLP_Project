Q1: What culture's arrival in Scotland is known as the "Davidian Revolution"?
A1: Norman
Q2: According to Document 1, where did earlier Viking settlers establish colonies?
A2: Earlier Viking settlers established colonies in the east (Roumois and Pays de Caux) around the low Seine valley and in the west in the Cotentin Peninsula.
Q3: In what regions did Rollo's contingents settle according to Document 1?
A3: Rollo's contingents settled Normandy and parts of the Atlantic coast.
Q4: What does the term "Afranji" refer to in the context of Document 2?
A4: Afranji refers to a castle named after the ethnicity of its Norman builders, meaning "Franks."
Q5: What was the significance of the treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte according to Document 3?
A5: The treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte established the Duchy of Normandy as a fiefdom for Rollo and his men in exchange for their protection against further Viking incursions.
