Q1: What place had the Norman Arab architectural style?
A1: The Kingdom of Sicily.
Q2: Which regions' populations did not differ before Rollo's arrival according to Document 1?
A2: Picardy and the Île-de-France.
Q3: Who were the earlier Viking settlers mentioned in Document 1?
A3: Danes, Norwegians, Norse-Gaels, Orkney Vikings, possibly Swedes, and Anglo-Danes from the English Danelaw under Norse control.
Q4: In which empire did Norman mercenaries fight alongside Varangian and Lombard contingents during the Sicilian campaign of George Maniaces?
A4: The Byzantine Empire.
Q5: When were the two customaries in Latin for the customary law of Normandy written?
A5: Between 1200 and 1245 for the Très ancien coutumier, and between 1235 and 1245 for the Grand coutumier de Normandie.
