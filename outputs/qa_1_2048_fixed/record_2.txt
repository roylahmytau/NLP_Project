Q1: From which countries did the Norse originate?
A1: Denmark, Iceland and Norway
Q2: What significant event occurred in 1107 involving Bohemond and the Normans?
A2: Bohemond, under the command of Robert's son, led the Normans in landing in Valona and besieging Dyrrachium but was forced to retreat and sign a peace treaty with the Byzantines.
Q3: What notable work of Norman art is mentioned in one of the documents?
A3: The Bayeux Tapestry, which is a work of embroidery.
Q4: How did the Norman influence in England change after the conquest of 1066?
A4: The Normans and their descendants replaced the Anglo-Saxons as the ruling class of England, and early Norman kings of England, as Dukes of Normandy, owed homage to the King of France for their land on the continent.
