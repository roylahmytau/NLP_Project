Q1: Who were the Normans descended from?
A1: The Normans were descended from Norse raiders and pirates from Denmark, Iceland, and Norway.

Q2: What is the name of the document that discusses the Norman invasion of Ireland?
A2: Document 3 discusses the Norman invasion of Ireland.

Q3: In which document is the conquest of Cyprus mentioned?
A3: Document 4 mentions the conquest of Cyprus.

Q4: Which two individuals did Richard name as governors of Cyprus before leaving?
A4: Richard named Richard de Camville and Robert de Thornham as governors of Cyprus.

Q5: According to Document 1, when were the Très ancien coutumier and the Grand coutumier de Normandie written?
A5: The Très ancien coutumier was authored between 1200 and 1245, and the Grand coutumier de Normandie was authored between 1235 and 1245.
