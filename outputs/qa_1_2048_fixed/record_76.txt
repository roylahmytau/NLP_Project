Q1: What territories did the Duchy of Normandy initially cover?
A1: The Duchy of Normandy initially covered the northern part of present-day Upper Normandy down to the river Seine.
Q2: Who were the Normans and how did they become associated with Normandy?
A2: The Normans were descendants of Norse raiders and pirates from Denmark, Iceland, and Norway who agreed to swear fealty to King Charles III of West Francia under their leader Rollo. Over generations, they assimilated with the native Frankish and Roman-Gaulish populations and established Normandy.
Q3: What significant historical event marked the beginning of Norman rule in England?
A3: Duke William II of Normandy conquered England in 1066, killing King Harold II at the Battle of Hastings.
