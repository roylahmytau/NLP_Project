Q1: What significant event marked the beginning of Western European domination of Cyprus?
A1: The conquest of Cyprus by the Anglo-Norman forces of the Third Crusade.
Q2: Which treaty established the Duchy of Normandy, and what was its main purpose?
A2: The treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte between King Charles III of West Francia and Rollo established the Duchy of Normandy, offering protection against further Viking incursions.
Q3: How did the Normans' identity evolve over time according to Document 2?
A3: The distinct cultural and ethnic identity of the Normans emerged initially in the first half of the 10th century and continued to evolve over succeeding centuries through assimilation and mixing with the native Frankish and Roman-Gaulish populations.
Q4: What linguistic changes occurred due to the influence of Anglo-Norman?
A4: The Anglo-Norman language became distinct from Latin and was eventually absorbed into Old English, influencing its development into Middle English.
Q5: Which treaty and location are associated with the establishment of the Duchy of Normandy?
A5: The treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte between King Charles III of West Francia and Rollo, and the river Epte.
