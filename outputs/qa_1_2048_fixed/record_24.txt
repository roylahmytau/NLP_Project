Q1: What were the origins of the Raoulii family?
A1: The Raoulii were descended from an Italo-Norman named Raoul.

Q2: Which historical period saw the recruitment of Norman mercenaries by Byzantine emperors?
A2: The period of the Comnenian Restoration saw the recruitment of Norman mercenaries by Byzantine emperors.

Q3: How did the Normans first attempt to engage with Wales?
A3: The Normans initially failed to make any headway into Wales when they were tasked with defending the Marches and warring with the Welsh by Edward the Confessor's earl of Hereford, Ralph.

Q4: Name a Norman participant in the War of Barbastro.
A4: William of Montreuil led the papal army and participated in the War of Barbastro.

Q5: What was the primary goal of the expedition led by Jean de Bethencourt and Gadifer de la Salle?
A5: The primary goal of the expedition led by Jean de Bethencourt and Gadifer de la Salle was to conquer the Canarian islands of Lanzarote, Fuerteventura, and El Hierro.
