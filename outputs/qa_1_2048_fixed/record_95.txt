Q1: What title did Bethencourt take in relation to the Canary Islands?
A1: King of the Canary Islands.
Q2: Which document mentions the Norman conquest of England?
A2: Document 2.
Q3: What language did the Normans adopt after settling in the Frankish land?
A3: Norman, Normaund or Norman French.
Q4: Where did earlier Viking settlers divide their colonies?
A4: Between the east (Roumois and Pays de Caux) around the low Seine valley and in the west in the Cotentin Peninsula.
Q5: What achievement is attributed to Prince Bohemond I?
A5: He founded the Principality of Antioch in the Levant.
