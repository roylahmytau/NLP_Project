Q1: What islands did Jean de Bethencourt and Gadifer de la Salle conquer between 1402 and 1405?
A1: Lanzarote, Fuerteventura and El Hierro.

Q2: How did the French Wars of Religion and the French Revolution affect Norman architecture and art?
A2: The French Wars of Religion caused the wanton destruction of many Norman edifices, while the French Revolution caused the purposeful destruction of religious objects and resulted in rampant pillaging.

Q3: Which Norman ruler formed a close relationship with King Ethelred II of England through marriage?
A3: Duke Richard II of Normandy.

Q4: What significant event occurred in 1066 that involved Norman rule?
A4: The Norman conquest of England at the Battle of Hastings.

Q5: Who founded the Principality of Antioch in the Levant during the Crusades?
A5: Bohemond I, a Norman prince.
