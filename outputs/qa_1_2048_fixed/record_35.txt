Q1: What was the title taken by Bethencourt regarding the Canary Islands?
A1: King of the Canary Islands.
Q2: In what century did the distinct cultural and ethnic identity of the Normans emerge?
A2: The distinct cultural and ethnic identity of the Normans emerged initially in the first half of the 10th century.
Q3: Which two individuals were responsible for developing and teaching the system of denoting notes by letters at Fécamp Abbey?
A3: William of Volpiano and John of Ravenna.
Q4: What was the name of the customaries written in Latin by two judges for use by them and their colleagues in Normandy?
A4: The Très ancien coutumier and the Grand coutumier de Normandie.
Q5: Who were the two Norman generals appointed by Richard as governors of Cyprus after its conquest?
A5: Richard de Camville and Robert de Thornham.
