Q2: How did the Normans contribute to the Crusades?
A2: Many Normans from Italy, France, and England served as avid Crusaders under the Italo-Norman prince Bohemund I and the Anglo-Norman king Richard the Lion-Heart.

A3: The Raoulii.
Q4: Where did Richard the Lion-hearted's fleet arrive after the storm?
A4: Limassol on Cyprus.
Q5: Who was Richard the Lion-hearted's sister and fiancée who were on the boat that was anchored on the south coast of Cyprus?
