Q1: What significant effect did the Normans have on Irish culture?
A1: The Normans had a profound effect on Irish culture and history, becoming "more Irish than the Irish themselves."

Q2: Which document discusses the origins of the Duchy of Normandy?
A2: Document 2 discusses the origins of the Duchy of Normandy.

Q3: How did the names of Norman descendants differ from those of other groups in Ireland?
A3: Norman descendants could be recognized by names such as French, (De) Roche, Devereux, D'Arcy, Treacy, Lacy, Furlong, Fitzgerald, FitzGibbons, Fitzmaurice, Barry, and De B\u00farca (Burke).

Q4: What was the significance of the Treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte?
A4: The Treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte offered Rollo and his men French lands in exchange for protection against further Viking incursions, leading to the establishment of the Duchy of Normandy.

Q5: What is the nature of the Bayeux Tapestry, despite its name?
A5: The Bayeux Tapestry is a work of embroidery, not a true tapestry.
