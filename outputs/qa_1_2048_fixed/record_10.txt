Q1: When was the Duchy of Normandy founded?
A1: 911

Q2: Who commissioned the Bayeux Tapestry?
A2: Odo, the Bishop of Bayeux and first Earl of Kent.

Q3: What event led to the third attack on Dyrrachium in 1185?
A3: A large Norman army invaded Dyrrachium due to the betrayal of high Byzantine officials.

Q4: What title did Jean's nephew acquire regarding the Canary Islands?
A4: King of the Canary Islands, as vassal to Henry III of Castile.

Q5: What region did the Duchy of Normandy originally cover?
A5: The northern part of present-day Upper Normandy down to the river Seine.
