Q1: In what year did the treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte establish the Duchy of Normandy?
A1: 911

Q2: According to Document 3, why did the Norman army invade Dyrrachium in 1185?
A2: Due to the betrayal of high Byzantine officials.

Q3: What territories were initially offered to Rollo under the treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte?
A3: The French lands between the river Epte and the Atlantic coast.

Q4: Who sold the rights to the Canary Islands to Enrique Pérez de Guzmán, 2nd Count de Niebla?
A4: Maciot de Bethencourt.

Q5: Which two years are mentioned in relation to Roger de Tosny's attempt to establish a state in the Iberian Peninsula?
