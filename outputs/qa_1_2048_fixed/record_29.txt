Q1: Who was the person that led the Normans in their campaign against the Byzantines in 1107?
A1: Bohemond.

Q2: What happened to Isaac after Richard's conquest of Cyprus?
A2: Isaac surrendered and was confined with silver chains.

Q3: In what century did the Très ancien coutumier and the Grand coutumier de Normandie get transcribed?
A3: Between 1200 and 1245.

Q4: Which abbey was known for developing the system of denoting notes by letters in the 11th century?
A4: Fécamp Abbey.

Q5: What event contributed significantly to Richard's reputation after he conquered Cyprus?
A5: His exploit of conquering the whole island.
