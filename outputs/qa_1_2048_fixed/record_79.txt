Q1: What kind of arches does Norman architecture have?
A1: Rounded arches.
Q2: How did the Norman conquest of Cyprus impact its history?
A2: The conquest opened a new chapter in the history of the island, leading to Western European domination for 380 years.
Q3: In what ways did the Normans influence the development of the English language?
A3: The Anglo-Norman language influenced the evolution of Old English, contributing to the development of Middle English and ultimately Modern English.
Q4: What were some of the places occupied by the Normans during their campaign in Albania?
A4: The Normans occupied Petrela, the citadel of Mili, Gllavenica (Ballsh), Kanina, and Jericho.
Q5: During which historical event did the Normans first land in Valona?
A5: The Normans first landed in Valona a few years after the First Crusade, in 1107.
