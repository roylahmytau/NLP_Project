Q5: What was the significance of the Bayeux Tapestry according to Document 13?
A5: It is the most famous work of Norman art and was commissioned by Odo, the Bishop of Bayeux and first Earl of Kent, employing natives from Kent who were learned in the Nordic traditions imported in the previous half century by the Danish Vikings.
