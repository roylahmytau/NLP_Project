Q1: Who conquered England in 1066?
A1: Duke William II of Normandy.

Q2: What event marked the beginning of Norman rule in England?
A2: The Battle of Hastings in 1066.

Q3: How did the Norman invasion affect the social structure of England?
A3: The invading Normans and their descendants replaced the Anglo-Saxons as the ruling class of England.

Q4: Which Scottish king played a significant role in introducing Normans and Norman culture to Scotland?
A4: King David I of Scotland.

Q5: What was the impact of the Norman dynasty on medieval Europe?
A5: The Norman dynasty had a major political, cultural, and military impact on medieval Europe and even the Near East.
