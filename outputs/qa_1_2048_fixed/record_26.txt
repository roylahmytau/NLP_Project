Q1: When did Dyrrachium fall to the Normans?
A1: February 1082.
Q2: Who betrayed Dyrrachium to the Normans?
A2: The Venetian and Amalfitan merchants who had settled there.
Q3: What happened to Dyrrachium after the Normans lost it in 1085?
A3: It was recaptured by the Byzantines.
Q4: Who led the Norman army that invaded Dyrrachium in 1081?
A4: Robert Guiscard.
Q5: Which other city, besides Dyrrachium, did the Normans capture in 1081?
A5: Valona.
