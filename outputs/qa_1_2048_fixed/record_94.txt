Q1: Who was the leader of the Normans who agreed to swear fealty to King Charles III of West Francia?
A1: Rollo.
Q2: In which region did the Normans initially give their name to, according to Document 1?
A2: Normandy.
Q3: What architectural features are characteristic of Norman architecture, as mentioned in Document 3?
A3: Rounded arches, particularly over windows and doorways, and massive proportions.
Q4: According to Document 4, where did the monks from Saint-Evroul establish a Latin monastery?
A4: At Sant'Eufemia.
Q5: Who encouraged the Normans to enter Italy initially, according to Document 2?
