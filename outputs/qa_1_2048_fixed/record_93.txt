Q1: What monastery did the Saint-Evroul monks establish in Italy?
A1: A Latin monastery at Sant'Eufemia.

Q2: In which document is the establishment of the Sant'Eufemia monastery mentioned?
A2: Document 4.

Q3: Who supported the monks of Saint-Evroul when they fled to southern Italy?
A3: Robert Guiscard.

Q4: What was the significance of the choir at Saint Evroul?
A4: The choir achieved fame in Normandy.

Q5: Which abbot initiated the migration of monks from Saint Evroul to Italy?
A5: Robert de Grantmesnil.
