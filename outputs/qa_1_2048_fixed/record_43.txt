Q1: When did Duke William II of Normandy conquer England?
A1: In 1066.
Q2: What is the most famous work of Norman art mentioned in the documents?
A2: The Bayeux Tapestry.
Q3: Which document discusses the development of customary law in Normandy?
A3: Document 3.
Q4: How did the early Norman kings of England view their English kingdom compared to their holdings in Normandy?
A4: They considered England to be their most important holding.
Q5: Who commissioned the Bayeux Tapestry?
A5: Odo, the Bishop of Bayeux and first Earl of Kent.
