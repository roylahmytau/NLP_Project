Q1: What architectural feature was characteristic of Norman architecture?
A1: Rounded arches, particularly over windows and doorways.
Q2: How did the Normans affect the military landscape of the regions they subdued?
A2: By encastellating these regions with keeps in their north French style.
Q3: Which two events in the 16th and 18th centuries destroyed much of Norman architectural and artistic remnants?
A3: The French Wars of Religion and the French Revolution.
Q4: What was the outcome of the Normans' initial attempts to engage with Wales?
A4: The Normans failed to make any headway into Wales.
Q5: Who elevated Robert Guiscard to the dignity of count of Apulia?
A5: Pope Gregory VII.
