Q1: In which century did the Normans begin to enter the Byzantine Empire?
A1: 9th century

Q2: Who commissioned the Bayeux Tapestry according to Document 8?
A2: Odo, the Bishop of Bayeux and first Earl of Kent

Q3: What was the relationship between the Normans and the Anglo-Saxons after the Norman Conquest?
A3: The Normans and their descendants replaced the Anglo-Saxons as the ruling class of England.

Q4: Which Normans are mentioned to have taken up service with the Armenian state in Cilicia and the Taurus Mountains?
A4: A Norman named Oursel and his force of "Franks" led by Raimbaud.

Q5: What is a distinctive feature of Norman architecture as described in Document 11?
A5: Rounded arches, particularly over windows and doorways, and massive proportions.
