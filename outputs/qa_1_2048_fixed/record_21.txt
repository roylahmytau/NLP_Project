Q1: What was the name of the Norman castle?
A1: Afranji
Q2: Which city was under Norman rule in Italy where Italo-Normans might have been present?
A2: Amalfi or Bari
Q3: In what year did Robert Guiscard lead an army to capture Valona, Kanina, and Jericho (Orikumi)?
A3: 1081
Q4: Who initially held the city of Dyrrachium before it was betrayed to the Normans?
A4: The Venetian and Amalfitan merchants who had settled there.
Q5: What was the significance of the name "Afranji" for the castle built by the Normans?
A5: It means "Franks."
